Find Out Your Akan Name

In this form you will be able to acquire specific details about what your Akan name is. Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture. Frequently in Ghana,children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Details

This section will be able to assist people to enter their birthdays and also to help those who do not know their specific name on the day they were born. Below in the input "Gender" you will be required to enter the letter M (meaning male) and F (meaning Female) to get your specific details. In the Year input you will be required to type in the year you were born.
